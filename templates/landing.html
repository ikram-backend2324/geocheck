How a check-in happens

Three signal stages, start to finish.

01 · Arm

Turn on location

The user opens Perimetr and grants GPS access once. Nothing is sent anywhere until they actually move toward a zone.

02 · Cross

Enter the perimeter

The moment their coordinates fall inside a zone you've drawn, the server re-checks the math itself — so it can't be spoofed from the browser.

03 · Ping

Control room is notified

Username and exact timestamp land in the live feed within seconds — no page refresh, no polling app, nothing to install.

The boundary, in 3D

A geofence is just a dome around a point.

Each zone is a center point and a radius. Anything that crosses under the dome counts as "inside" — and triggers a signal up to the control room.
